Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro

Cristiano Ronaldo's current team is Al Nassr as of 03/10/2024

1985 - Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro is born on February 5 in Funchal, Madeira, Portugal. 2003 - Ronaldo makes his senior debut for Sporting CP in Portugal. 2003 - Ronaldo makes his international debut for Portugal at the age of 18. 2003 - Ronaldo signs with Manchester United, starting his career in the English Premier League. 2004 - Ronaldo scores his first international goal for Portugal at Euro 2004. 2008 - Ronaldo captains Portugal for the first time. 2009 - Ronaldo transfers to Real Madrid for a then-world record fee. 2013 - Ronaldo wins his first Ballon d'Or while playing for Real Madrid. 2016 - Ronaldo leads Portugal to victory in Euro 2016, their first major international trophy. 2018 - Ronaldo moves to Juventus in another high-profile transfer. 2019 - Ronaldo wins the UEFA Nations League with Portugal. 2021 - Ronaldo returns to Manchester United, where he started his career. 2022 - Ronaldo's contract with Manchester United is terminated. 2023 - Ronaldo signs with Al Nassr, a club in the Saudi Pro League.
